Venus, The Scorcher

Venus is the second planet from the sun, and has the longest day of any planet, at 243 earth days to complete one rotation around its axis. The environment of venus is extremely ill suited to life as we know it, due to the runaway greenhouse gas effect which has been observed, causing it to be the hottest planet in the solar system, with its suface at 863 oF. Due to the ability to observe venus with the naked eye, it has been know by many ancient cultures, calling it the morning star and evening star due to its visibility as the sun rises and sets.
