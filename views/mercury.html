Mercury, The Sprinter

Mercury is the smallest planet in our solar system, and also the planet which orbits closest to the sun. As such, it has the shortest year of all the planets, completing a full orbit in only 88 days. Due to its proximity, the sunlight on the surface would be over 5x brighter than the sunlight which reaches us on earth. The planet is named after the Roman god Mercury, the messenger of the gods.
