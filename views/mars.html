Mars, The Red Planet

Mars is the fourth planet from the sun, and has a distinct red color due to the iron oxide which makes up the planet's surface. Mars is named after the Roman god of war. The planet is smaller than earth, but has two moons, Phobos and Deimos, which orbit the planet. Mars has been in news recently as the NASA mars rover Curiosity has made many discoveries, including the presence of water beneath the surface of the planet.
